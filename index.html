Hi. I’m
Srived Majumder

A passionate Director and Editor who is working in this field for 5+ years. Srived Majumder, is a 24 year old filmmaker trained in India and Calgary, Alberta, with a two-year advanced diploma in advance virtual film production. Skilled in building immersive Unreal Engine sets and producing films on virtual stages, he has created over five acclaimed film projects, with selections at festivals worldwide, including Rome. Known for delivering ambitious superhero and action films on minimal budgets, Srived has led crews of up to 200 people and founded a production company creating films, music videos, and more. His work blends technology and emotion to craft stories that resonate globally.
